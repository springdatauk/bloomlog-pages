Restore Pro purchase

If you bought BloomLog Pro and the App does not show Pro features:

  • Open BloomLog → Settings and use Restore Purchases (or equivalent), if shown.
  • Confirm you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the purchase.
  • Check Settings → Apple ID → Media & Purchases on your device for purchase history.

If it still fails, email us at the address above with a short description; we cannot access your Apple receipts directly, but we can help you rule out common issues.

Your data

BloomLog keeps your plants, About this plant text, dated Journal entries, harvests, and optional photos on your device. We do not host your garden data on our servers. If you replace your device, use Apple’s device backup or migration tools if you want to move app data to a new iPhone or iPad.
